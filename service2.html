the service includes:

01
Detention Ponds

After Hurricane Harvey flooded the Greater Houston area, the county has increased the detention requirements for new construction. Dry detention ponds temporarily old runoff and slowly release and help filter the water to the county and city storm system.

02
Retention Ponds

This is what people think of when they say pond. These are designed to retain the water. The design is important to ensure managed algae growth and proper heat & oxygen management if you want to have fish or similar in your pond.

03
Natural Swimming Ponds

These ponds are a growing trend for people wanting to ditch the harsh chemicals and expensive upkeep of a swimming pool. These ponds are lined and filtered to give you clean and clear water.
